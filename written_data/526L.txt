spoken

Inside the veil!

Precious as the blood

that bought us,

Perfect, as the love that sought
us,

Holy, as the Lamb that brought us
Inside the veil.

When we see Thy love unshaken
Outside the camp;
Scorned by man, by God
forsaken

Outside the camp;

Thy loved cross alone
can charm us,

Shame need now

no more alarm us,

Glad we follow,

nought can harm us
Outside the camp.

Lamb of God,

through Thee we enter
Inside the veil.

Cleansed by Thee,

we boldly venture

Inside the veil.

Not a stain; a new creation:
Ours is such a full salvation;
Low we bow in adoration
Inside the veil.

Unto Thee,

the homeless stranger

Outside the camp,

Forth we hasten,

fear no danger

Outside the camp.

Than all Egypt's
